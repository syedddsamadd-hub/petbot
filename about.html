FAQs

What are PeetBot bottles? VIP +
PeetBot bottles are premium quality reusable bottles that are perfect for keeping your drinks cold or hot. They are BPA-free, leak-proof, and come with a stylish design. Available in different sizes and colors!
How long does the bottle keep water cold? +
Our insulated bottles can keep water cold for up to 24 hours and hot for up to 12 hours. Double-wall vacuum insulation technology is used to maintain the temperature.
What material are the bottles made of? +
PeetBot bottles are made of high-grade stainless steel (304 or 316) which is rust-proof and durable. They are 100% BPA-free and use food-grade certified materials that are completely safe for health.
How to clean the bottle? VIP +
Wash the bottle daily with warm water and mild soap. For deep cleaning, you can use a baking soda and vinegar solution. It is also dishwasher safe, but hand wash is recommended for longer life. Clean the cap and rubber seals separately.
Can the bottles be placed in the dishwasher? +
Yes, but hand washing is better. If using a dishwasher, place it on the top rack and use a gentle cycle. This will protect the bottle‚Äôs coating and insulation.

Are the jars air-tight? +
Absolutely! Our jars have premium quality silicone seals that provide 100% air-tight protection. They protect your food items from moisture, pests, and air while maintaining freshness.
What sizes are available for jars? +
Our jars are available in multiple sizes: 250ml (for small spices), 500ml (medium storage), 1L (large storage), and 2L (bulk storage). You can choose according to your needs.
Are glass jars microwave safe? VIP +
The glass body is microwave and oven safe, but the lid must be removed because it is not heat resistant. Always check that there are no metal parts in the jar before microwaving.
Are PeetBot bottles safe for carbonated drinks? VIP +
Yes, PeetBot bottles are completely safe for carbonated beverages like soda and sparkling water. Pressure-tested caps ensure carbonation is maintained and prevent leakage.
Are acidic drinks like juice or lemonade safe? +
Absolutely! Stainless steel material can handle acidic beverages without any corrosion or taste transfer. You can safely store juice, lemonade, tea, and coffee.
How long will hot beverages stay hot? +
In insulated bottles, hot beverages can stay hot for up to 12 hours. Fill coffee or tea in the morning and enjoy it until noon! Perfect for office, travel, or outdoor activities.
